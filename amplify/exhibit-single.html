Lauren YS: Phantasmagoria

02.01 - 03.01

Artist Lauren YS catapults her audience into a world of brilliantly vibrant colours, hypnotizing motion, and complex form with her solo exhibition 'Phantasmagoria'. Showcasing fantastical creatures and heroines, often enmeshed in one another, YS explores surreal absurdity with this collection of various works.

Lauren YS is an illustrator and muralist currently residing in the Bay Area. With a BA in English and Art Practice from Stanford, YS has exhibited her work in galleries across the US as well as International cities including Vienna, London, Auckland, Melbourne, and Sydney.

Free to Attend
