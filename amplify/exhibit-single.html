FEBRUARY 15TH, 4:30PM - 5:20PM | $14 - $30

When you buy that first tube of paint it gives you an artist license. It's a good way of getting rid of all your anxieties and hostilities. Let's get crazy. It's so important to do something every day that will make you happy. Nothing wrong with washing your brush. All you have to do is let your imagination go wild.

Don't fight it, use what happens. Just think about these things in your mind - then bring them into your world. This is your world. These trees are so much fun. I get started on them and I have a hard time stopping. Everyone is going to see things differently - and that's the way it should be.
