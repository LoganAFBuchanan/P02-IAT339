About Us

A brief summary

Ampl!fy is your one stop shop for local, intriguing, beguiling art. We have a display space of over 1000 sq ft and have the largest alumni of local artists in B.C.. Whether you want to peruse our diverse collection, or simply talk with your fellow art lovers, Ampl!fy provides a space to engage with the art community in any form it may come.

Mission Statement

Ampl!fy is a movement against waiting for opportunity. Instead we seek to build the space we wanted (needed!) within the art community. Ampl!fy fights against the onslaught of complacency in a world beset by the cowardly creature comforts of the ordinary.

Accessible Pricing

Ampl!fy is committed to disrupting the structures imposed upon the art world. We aim to make art accessible, financially or otherwise, to anyone who is curious enough to walk through our doors. All of our exhibitions are free to the public and have a strictly open door policy, the only restriction is the capacity of our building.
