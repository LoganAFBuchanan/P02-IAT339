Russell Alton

www.choplogik.org

Russell Alton, also known by his alternative title Chop-Logik, is an illustrator and tattoo artist sprouted in Smithers BC before being replanted in Vancouver BC. His work is characterized by the wonderous world he creates that merges our reality and his whimsical imaginings, filled with cheeky Gleep-Glops and jaunty crystal wizards.

Alton has exhibited his work in galleries across BC and throughout the US, with pieces residing in collector's homes across the world.
