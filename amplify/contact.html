Ways to Reach Us

The most obvious way to get to know us is to stop on by, we’re always happy to discuss upcoming exhibits and local artists. But, if for some reason you want a little distance, you can give us a call and we’ll fill you in on any details you need.

Accessibility Info

Our gallery is fully wheelchair/scooter/stroller accessible. If you require any further assistance please don’t hesitate to let us know of any extra considerations you require and we will happily make the needed preparations.

Please let us know at least 24 hours before your event as we need a bit of set up time.

Submit Work/Proposals

We are always looking for new pieces to display in our gallery. Our gallery space is quite versatile and can support a range of installations. Our past exhibitions have ranged from small framed displays to full room installations and everything in between. Be aware that our calendar fills up fast so applying early is encouraged.

If you want to get a spot on our walls/floor/ceiling then send us an email at submissions@amplify.ca.
