Artist Talk: Nomi Chi

February 15th, 4:30pm - 5:20pm | $14 - $30

Nomi Chi, illustrator and tattoo artist, shares their experiences throughout their artistic journey in this 50 minute talk. Join us to hear Nomi's measured insights on studying art in an academic context during their time at the Emily Carr University, developing an artistic voice, and navigating a commercial practice.

Nomi Chi is a multidisplinary visual artist living in Vancouver, BC. They hold a BFA in illustration from Emily Carr University of Art and Design and have exhibited their work in galleries across the world, including Canada, the US, and Europe.
